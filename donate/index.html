✨ Who Are We? ✨
We’re Hope on Wheels, a foundation so committed, we’d sell our souls (if we hadn’t already) to make life better for orphans and differently-abled kids. We take the broken toys of society and turn them into unstoppable forces. Call us the real-life patch notes for humanity.

🌈 What Do We Do? 🌈
We:
🚴‍♀️Help orphans so they don’t just cycle through life aimlessly.
🦽Support differently-abled kids because rolling over obstacles isn’t just a metaphor—it’s their daily grind.
🎒Educate them because no child should ever be left asking, “What’s 9+10?”

In short, we’re like duct tape: messy, chaotic, but we hold lives together.
